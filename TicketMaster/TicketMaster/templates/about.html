About Easy Ticket


The “Easy Ticket” Ticket Management System is a secure, lightweight ticket management solution for small IT teams that doesn’t want the complexity, steep learning curves, or the expense of larger commercial solutions. Focused on ease of use, the system is optimized for the four core activities necessary in any ticket management system: creating new tickets, updating existing tickets as the issue evolves, closing tickets when the issue is resolved, and searching both open and closed tickets to update progress or reference past issues and solutions. With a responsive and minimal interface specifically designed for a minimal learning curve and reducing the time it takes to deal with tickets, technicians can spend more time fixing issues and less time struggling to learn or remember how their ticketing system works.
